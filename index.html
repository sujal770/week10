What makes our furniture special

Science meet design

Science and design principles are often employed in furniture creation to enhance ergonomics, sustainability, durability, and aesthetics. Materials, such as wood, metal, plastic, and fabrics, are carefully selected and combined to achieve the desired look and function of the piece.

Maximal comfort

The design elements that are considered in maximal comfort furniture include cushioning, ergonomic shapes, soft textures, and adjustable features. The furniture is often designed to distribute the user's weight evenly and support the natural curves of their body.

Ethnical and sustainable

This type of furniture is often made from sustainable, non-toxic, and eco-friendly materials, such as FSC-certified wood, bamboo, cork, and recycled or upcycled materials. The production process is typically transparent, and fair labor practices are prioritized to ensure that workers are treated fairly and paid living wages.
